About Cavit.

My name is Cavit Karakuş . I was born in 2001 in Muğla. I completed my primary education at Atatürk Primary School in 2014. I took part in the Kavaklıdere Field Hockey team with my interest in sports activities and team games in primary school. In this process, together with my team, I achieved the third place success in Muğla. I got second place in Mangala competitions, which is a strategic game in secondary school. Then I graduated from Kavaklıdere Anatolian High School in 2019. Meanwhile, as the high school basketball team, we won the third place in a competition held throughout the province. I continued my education in Mersin University Erdemli Applied Technology and Business School Computer Technology and Information Systems department.

The main reason for me to choose this profession is my interest in informatics and my sister is a mechatronics engineer. During my high school years, I fell in love with the computer and informatics industry. I wanted to get my education in this field, which I also love.

During my undergraduate education, I received training on general business, general accounting, economics, occupational health and safety.

In the meantime, I attended the Hacknbreak Open Innovation Camp held in Izmir and completed Linux, Web design, Unity education, various professional expertise and certification programs. Following my ISO 9001-2000 Quality Management System studies, I have accumulated the necessary equity capital to establish my own company and to carry out greenhouse automation design and production with my own business.
